Gameplay
MANIFESTO! is a game for individuals or groups of 2-6 players. For each round, player(s) are dealt 1 random card from each of 4 categories: Provocation, Orientation, Opening, Tone. (One substitution is allowed.)
  • Provocation gives you the topic or theme of your manifesto;
  • Orientation tells you the type of manifesto youâ€™re creating;
  • Opening gives you the first words;
  • Tone is the tone you take towards your subject or audience.
These are your 4 constraints: use them as a prompt to write or draw your manifesto. When you finish a round vote for the best manifesto. Caution will get you nowhere! Share photos of your #manifestogame on Twitter (@words_project) or Instagram (@wordsinfreedomproject), or better yet use them to start a revolution of your own.
